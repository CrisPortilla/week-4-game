A random number will be given to you at the beginning of the game, called the winning number.

These are the four stones that have appeared so far in the Marvel Universe! When one is clicked you will be given a random number associated with that stone.

In order to win the game, your total score must match the winning number that is given to you at the start of the game. You lose if your total score goes over the random number!

In every start of the game, the value of the stones get randomized. The value of the stone is displayed in the total score, when clicked on.
